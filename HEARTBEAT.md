# HEARTBEAT.md - Autonomous Check-in

_What should your agent check when it has a heartbeat (periodic self-check)?_
_Keep this small — it runs frequently._

---

## Quick Checks

- [ ] _(Example: Any urgent emails?)_
- [ ] _(Example: Calendar events in next 2 hours?)_
- [ ] _(Example: Any pending tasks?)_

---

## When to Reach Out

- _(Example: Important email arrived)_
- _(Example: Calendar event in < 2 hours)_
- _(Example: Something interesting found)_

## When to Stay Quiet

- _(Example: Late night unless urgent)_
- _(Example: Nothing new since last check)_
